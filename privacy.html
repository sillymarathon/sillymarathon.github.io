Your Privacy Matters to Us

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 16, 2025

Marathon 2025 is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you register for our event.

1. Information We Collect

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Age
  • Blood Group
  • Selected Event Category

2. Purpose of Data Collection

We collect your information to:

  • Process your event registration.
  • Generate and deliver a QR code for identification and attendance marking.
  • Contact you for event updates, confirmations, and emergency communications.

3. Data Security

Your data is securely stored using encrypted cloud services and is accessible only to authorized personnel.

4. Data Retention

We retain your data until the event concludes. Data will be deleted or anonymized within 6 months unless required for compliance or audit purposes.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time (this may cancel your registration).

For requests, email: support@marathon2025.com

6. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated occasionally. Please check this page for the latest version.
